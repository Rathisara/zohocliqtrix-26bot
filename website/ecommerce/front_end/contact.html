Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all items in original condition with tags attached. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in their original packaging. Sale items may have different return conditions.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping typically takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping (2-3 business days) and overnight shipping are also available at checkout. Delivery times may vary during holidays or peak seasons.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. Additional customs fees or taxes may apply depending on your country's import regulations.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number and link. You can also track your order by logging into your account on our website and visiting the Order History section.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. All payments are processed securely through encrypted channels.
